MEDIA SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
========================

The webpage requires the following media files in the public folder:

1. F1 VIDEO (Hero Section)
   - Named: f1video.webm
   - Location: public/f1video.webm
   - Format: WebM video
   - Purpose: Background video in hero section

2. F1 AUDIO (Background Sound)
   - Named: f1audio.mp3 (or f1audio.webm, f1audio.ogg)
   - Location: public/f1audio.mp3
   - Format: MP3, WebM, or OGG audio
   - Purpose: Background ambient audio for the webpage
   
3. F1 NOTIFICATION SOUND
   - Named: f1notification.mp3 (or f1notification.webm, f1notification.ogg)
   - Location: public/f1notification.mp3
   - Format: MP3, WebM, or OGG audio
   - Purpose: Notification sound for events (team change, driver change, leaderboard update)

4. CHECKPOINT SOUND
   - Named: checkpoint.mp3 (or checkpoint.webm, checkpoint.ogg)
   - Location: public/checkpoint.mp3
   - Format: MP3, WebM, or OGG audio
   - Purpose: Plays when passing through sector checkpoints and demo triggers

5. HERO BACKGROUND IMAGE
   - Named: hero.jpg
   - Location: public/hero.jpg
   - Format: JPEG
   - Purpose: Faded background image for the container section

Audio Behavior:
- Background audio plays continuously in ambient mode
- Notification sound plays when:
  * Changing team selection
  * Changing driver selection
  * Leaderboard updates
- Background audio automatically mutes when session starts
- Background audio automatically unmutes when session ends
- Speaker button in top-right controls manual mute/unmute
- Audio starts muted by default due to browser autoplay policies
- First click anywhere on the page will attempt to start the audio

Once you add these files, all media features will work automatically.
